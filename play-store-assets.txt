LONG DESCRIPTION  (max 4000 chars)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mood Bible brings the comfort of Scripture to wherever you are emotionally.
Simply tap how you are feeling — Sad, Happy, Angry, Confused, or Unmotivated —
and the app opens a fresh set of King James Version verses handpicked for that
mood. A new selection is curated every day, so there is always something new
to reflect on.

KEY FEATURES

• Mood-based verse selection — five emotional categories, each with a
  carefully chosen collection of KJV scriptures.

• Daily rotation — verses are reshuffled each day using a date-based seed,
  giving you fresh encouragement every morning without repetition.

• Favourite verses — save any verse with one tap and revisit your personal
  collection whenever you need it. Favourites are stored privately on your
  device and never leave it.

• Beautiful book-style reader — an animated page-turn effect makes reading
  feel like leafing through a real Bible.

• Share instantly — send any verse to a friend via your phone's native share
  sheet, or copy it to the clipboard in one tap.

• Fully offline — no internet connection required. Open the app anywhere,
  any time.

• No ads, no accounts, no tracking — completely free with zero data
  collection. Your device, your data.

• Accessible design — clean typography, generous touch targets, and support
  for the system's reduced-motion setting.

All Bible text is from the King James Version (KJV), which is in the public
domain.

Whether you need a word of peace in a difficult moment, a verse of joy to
celebrate a blessing, or a reminder to stay patient and trust God's plan,
Mood Bible is your daily pocket companion to Scripture.
