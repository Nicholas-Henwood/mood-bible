Privacy Policy

Mood Bible App — Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Overview

Mood Bible App ("the App") is a free, offline Bible-verse companion app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data the App does and does not collect.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to any server
  • Financial or payment information
  • Any data from your contacts, camera, microphone, or files

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App uses your device's local storage (localStorage) solely to save Bible verses you mark as favourites. This data:

  • Never leaves your device.
  • Is never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

4. Internet Permissions

The App does not require an internet connection to function. If an internet permission is declared in the app manifest (for example, to enable the Share feature via Android's native share sheet), the App itself does not use that permission to contact any external server.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or social login providers. No third party receives any data from your use of the App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). Because no personal data is collected at all, no special handling is required.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
